🛡️ About Tactical FieldKit


        


          

            Tactical FieldKit is a privacy-first, 100% offline tool
            for generating structured situation reports (SITREPs) and sanitizing images of metadata.
            Built for law enforcement, journalists, NGO field workers, and security operators.
          


          

            ⚠️
            

              OPSEC Notice: This tool runs entirely in your browser.
              No data is transmitted, stored, or logged. For maximum security, use in
              Airplane Mode or on an air-gapped device. Clear your clipboard after use.
